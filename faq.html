Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do online Quran classes work at IlmSphere?
+

All classes take place via video call platforms such as Zoom or Google Meet. Once enrolled, you will be matched with a qualified teacher and agree on a suitable time slot. Your teacher will guide you through recitation, Tajweed corrections, and memorisation — all from the comfort of your home. Sessions are recorded on request for review.

Are the teachers at IlmSphere Academy qualified?
+

Absolutely. All our Quran teachers hold recognised Ijazah (certification) in recitation and Tajweed. Our academic tutors are qualified educators with relevant degrees and teaching experience, often with specific expertise in the UK curriculum. We conduct thorough background checks and ongoing training for all staff.

What age groups can enrol with IlmSphere Academy?
+

We welcome students of all ages — from young children (ages 5+) learning their first Quranic letters, through to adults beginning or continuing their Quran journey. Our academic tutoring serves students from primary school through to GCSE and A-Level preparation.

Do you offer flexible scheduling for working families?
+

Yes — flexibility is at the heart of what we offer. Lessons can be scheduled in the mornings, afternoons, evenings, and weekends to accommodate school timetables and family commitments. We understand that UK families are busy, and we work around your schedule, not the other way around.

Which countries can join IlmSphere Academy?
+

IlmSphere Academy is a fully online institution, meaning students from anywhere in the world are welcome. We primarily serve students and families in the UK, but we have students in Europe, North America, the Middle East, and South Asia. As long as you have a reliable internet connection, you can learn with us.

Can I try a lesson before committing to a plan?
+

Certainly. We offer a free trial lesson for all new students with no obligation to continue. This allows you to meet your potential teacher, experience our teaching style, and ensure IlmSphere is the right fit for your family before making any commitment.
